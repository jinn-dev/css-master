DECORATING WITH BOTANICALS
In an attempt to ignore the cold winter days, I started thinking about ways I can rush spring, and bring more greenery into my home. After our recent move from Northern California to Illinois, my beloved ferns, ficus tree and succulents got left behind so I needed other ways to get my green fix. For a while now I have noticed chic interiors with framed botanical prints and pressed herbals as favorites for interior designers so it’s safe to say that, following the overkill of the 80s, decorating with floral prints is trendy again.
